About Us

Pusat Sumber Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Bandar Puncak Jalil (PSS SMKBPUJ)

Here at SMKBPUJ, we place an intense focus on growth, honesty, and excellence in everything we do. In order to help student to realize their full academic, creative, and personal potential, our school is dedicated to create a secure, encouraging, and welcoming learning environment. We aim to inspire lifelong learners and responsible citizens with a committed staff of educators, state-of-the-art facilities, and a strong sense of community. Our students are encouraged to think critically, act compassionately, and lead with confidence whether they are on the field, in the classroom, or on stage.

Vision

To be a leading institution that empowers students to become innovative, compassionate, and responsible global citizens.

Mission

To provide a nurturing and dynamic learning environment that promotes academic excellence, personal integrity, and active citizenship.

Objectives

To provide high-quality, engaging instruction that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and a lifelong love of learning.
