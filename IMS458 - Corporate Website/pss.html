“Bersama Ayahanda: Menyemai Nilai & Inspirasi”

This event is a motivational and engagement session between students and a senior male educator, often referred to as ayahanda (a respectful term meaning “father figure” in Malay). The objective is to instill core values such as respect, discipline, and academic integrity while creating a space for students to interact closely with a role model. Held in a classroom setting, this session promotes personal development through storytelling, sharing life experiences, and academic encouragement.

“PSS Talk: Memperkasa Wacana Generasi Muda”

This event is a casual yet impactful talk session conducted in a modern learning space or resource center (Pusat Sumber Sekolah - PSS). The aim is to empower students through motivational speeches, open dialogue, and creative thinking forums. With students seated comfortably in a vibrant and relaxed environment, the event fosters inclusivity and encourages active participation, making it ideal for discussions on career paths, self-esteem, or digital literacy.

“Lawatan Edukasi Korporat: Menelusuri Dunia Profesional”

This educational visit gives students firsthand exposure to the corporate or industrial environment. Dressed in formal attire, they are guided by a professional speaker, likely from the host company or organization. The program includes guided tours, corporate culture insights, and potential career pathway briefings, designed to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world industry practices. It aims to broaden students’ horizons and prepare them for future career decisions.
