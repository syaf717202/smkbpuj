Special Spaces

Multimedia Room

A tech-equipped space where students can create and explore digital content through audio, video, and interactive media.

Screening Room

A small theater-style room used for viewing educational films, presentations, and student projects.

Study Room

A quiet and comfortable area designed for focused individual or group study.

Other Facilities and Services

Borrowing Books
Students can check out textbooks, novels, and reference books to support their learning and personal interests
Computer, Printing and Photostat
Computers with internet access are available for research, along with printing and photocopying services for schoolwork.
Informational Space
A quiet area stocked with newspapers, magazines, and reference materials kiosk to help students stay informed and expand their knowledge.
Discussion Space
A shared space where students can collaborate, exchange ideas, and work on group assignments or projects.
